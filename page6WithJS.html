A tributary,or affluent,is a stream or river that flows into a larger stream or main stem (or parent) river or a lake. A tributary does not flow directly into a sea or ocean.

A mountain is an elevated portion of the Earth's crust, generally with steep sides that show significant exposed bedrock.

A waterfall is a point in a river or stream where water flows over a vertical drop or a series of steep drops. Waterfalls also occur where meltwater drops over the edge of a tabular iceberg
